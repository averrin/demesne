Notation

This notation provides symbolic hints for differences between this (Improved) Guidebook and the original:

  • (*) a completely new chapter
  • (+) chapter contains additional information
  • (++) chapter contains a lot of additional and new information; deserves special attention
  • (m) chapter has been moved to this place from another location
  • (d) chapter was created by dividing the parent chapter
  • (r) chapter was renamed

Other sections may also contain minor additions, different wording or other small changes. Nicer tables or live links are not considered as additions for the above purpose.
