This particular map shows the result when a non-spellcaster enters 
the Library. There are scrolls located in the two large rooms. 
A spellcaster will find only spellbooks.

Note the guaranteed climbing set in the southwest corner.

The monster is:

G - the Ghost Librarian

There will be a random dangerous monster guarding every spellbook 
and scroll.

The #s are secret doors.
