The staircase in the central part of the map leads to the ogre cave after the 
PC has received the quest.

The (friendly) inhabitants are:

@ - Bart (always in the Green Griffon Inn)
@ - The Priest (always near the altar)
@ - Waldenbrook (always in his shop)
r - demented ratling
h - dwarven children
h - dwarven elite guardians
h - dwarven guardians
h - dwarven mystic (always in his sealed room)
h - dwarves and Torgall (always in the Green Griffon Inn)
h - Garth (always in his training hall)
h - Glod (always near his forge)
h - Thrundarr (always standing on the lever in his room)
