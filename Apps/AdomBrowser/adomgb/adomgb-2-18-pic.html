The color and location of the pools is random. 6-12 pools is typical.

The monsters are:

h - Kherab, the Dwarven Artificer
Y - steel golems

Also randomly found here:

[ - animated armors
h - dwarven chaos knights
Y - steel horrors
Y - steel zombies

The #s are secret doors.
