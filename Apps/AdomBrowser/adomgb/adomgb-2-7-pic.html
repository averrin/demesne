This level is heavily and randomly trapped in the labyrinth on the left.

The monsters are:

@ - Nonnak, the master necromancer
Y - a bone golem, guarding the elemental gauntlets
Z - Griff Bloodax, the undead dwarven chaos berserker
The + is Griff's grave, where the Sword of Nonnak can be found.

This level will contain a large variety of undead in the chamber on the right.

The #s are secret doors.
