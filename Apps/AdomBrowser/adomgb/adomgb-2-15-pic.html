The monsters in the central room stand on top of graves whose distribution 
is random. There may be an extra undead monster or two wandering about, 
as well as a few non-undead monsters.

The monsters are:

z - corpse fiends
z - ghuls
z - skeletons
z - zombies
G - ghosts
G - shadows
G - spectres
G - wraiths
L - lichs
Z - greater mummies
Z - mummies
Z - skeletal warriors
Z - vampires
Z - wights
